Principal Cloud Engineer with over a decade of experience across the SDLC, specialising in AWS, DevOps, and serverless architecture. I design and build cloud infrastructure for customers, and I talk about it on stage and on the podcast.

My default approach: if I've done something twice, I'll automate it the third time. Most of my work ends up being CI/CD, infrastructure-as-code, and monitoring — the stuff that keeps things running without someone having to babysit it.

Outside of work, I co-host LogiCast (a weekly AWS news podcast), run the AWS Brighton User Group, and am a 2x UK National Karate Champion (kata & kumite, 2025).

Logicata is an AWS Advanced Partner specialising in cloud consulting and managed services.
